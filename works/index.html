Online Riddles / Puzzle Games

EXEMPLAR BY BLOOD

(2022-Present)

Link: exemplarpuzzle.surge.sh

Exemplar By Blood is an online riddle about a mysterious case of a dead person suddenly releasing an online riddle two years after his passing. You get an email from Zaarei922734, a bubbly internet stranger who can’t solve online riddles and is asking for your help to solve her late younger brother’s mysterious online riddle.

The game uses poorly AI generated assets that have been edited manually for aesthetic appeal. Character illustrations featured were drawn by @laqry with his very unique artstyle.

The game is still a work in progress. The prologue is only accessible, with Act 1 still in development. It is expected to release this 2024.
