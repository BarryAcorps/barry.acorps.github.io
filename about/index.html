About Me

Hi, welcome to my About Me section. I’m trying to abridge this as much as possible so I don’t overshare, so perhaps I’ll just do sections as if I’m interviewed.

Tell Me About Yourself.

I’m Barry Acorps, but you can just call me Barry. Obviously, I’m a dude, but I’m still a minor. I’m from the Philippines 🇵🇭, and my birthday is on February 20 ♓.

In my free time I like to study STEM related subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science. When I’m not studying, I’m playing Rhythm Games, like Dance Dance Revolution or maimai; Precise Platformers or Metroidvanias, like Celeste or Hollow Knight; or Puzzle Games, which extend to actual puzzle games or online riddles.

I’ve been coding ever since I was 8, when I made my first ever HTML-based website. Nowadays, I’m using my skills to write stories and develop mediums for it. You can check out my works at my works page.
